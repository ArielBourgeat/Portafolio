Horror Movie Poster

Art Direction & Key Art

This key art explores a grounded horror mood using strong contrast, tight framing and minimal color. The goal was to create a poster that feels cinematic without overloading the composition.

Typography and composition were balanced to lead the eye from the title to the focal character, while textures and grain add a tactile layer that references classic horror posters.

The project iterates on several crops, lighting variations and text placement options to arrive at a final image that stands out both digitally and in print.
